Why we are open for sponsorship

We don’t run academicproductivity.com as a business. This is something we do as a passion. Running this website incurs a number of monetary costs, and one reason we look for sponsorship is to cover some of those costs. But the most important cost is not money. Of course, we place a high value on our time; writing valuable content, administering a server, keeping the blog spam-free, manage comments, etc does take a bite off our ability to do other things.
